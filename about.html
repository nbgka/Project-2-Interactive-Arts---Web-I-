Why we started

Include Me In The Pattern:

We are are non-profit website that makes efforts of resisting the negative and systematic issues of fatphobia in the fashion industry, especially when it comes to representing and making garments that fit mid-to-plus-size people. Our purpose is to provide free and accessible resources with downloadable free clothing patterns for mid-to-plus-size individuals, serving as a catalog with garment making resources and mid-to-plus size representation.

Include Me In The Pattern was developed and launched by a group of creatives and activists sharing the same goal: strive for size-inclusive clothing. With the demand for size-inclusivity from the fashion fashion industry growing stronger, Include Me In The Pattern was developed and launched in order to help push for accesibility of size-inclusive garments.
